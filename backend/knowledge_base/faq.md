# Frequently Asked Questions

## Purpose

This document provides broad customer support answers across booking, baggage, refunds, cancellations, check-in, visas, loyalty, meals, disruptions, and special assistance.

## How Customer Support Should Use This Document

- Use this article to answer general policy questions before checking booking-specific fare rules.
- When a customer's itinerary, ticket condition, immigration status, or disruption record changes the answer, verify the live reservation before giving a final response.
- Explain policy outcomes in plain language and avoid promising exceptions that require airport, revenue management, or regulatory approval.
- For safety, security, legal, and government-entry topics, direct the customer to official authorities when the final decision is outside airline control.

## General Booking Questions

This FAQ is designed for broad customer support retrieval. For detailed rules, retrieve the dedicated policy document after identifying the topic.

- Customers should provide booking reference, ticket number, passenger name, route, and travel date when asking for account-specific help.
- Policies may differ by operating airline, ticketing airline, fare, airport, and country.
- Support responses should distinguish general guidance from booking-specific decisions.
